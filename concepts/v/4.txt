Concept: Do
Note: There is a verb for almost all actions in Tani. This one is a general purpose verb.
Examples:
  - apatani/hari: "Nw mw[du]? = What do is? (What are you doing?)"
  - apatani/hari: "[Mw]saman [mw]yo = What's not to be done, don't do that (Don't do the forbidden)"
  - apatani/hari: "Nw [mw]du? = What doing? (What are you doing?)"
