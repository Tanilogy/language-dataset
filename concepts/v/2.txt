Concept: Is | Are | Exist
Note: Similar to the Japanese "desu", but used to refer others.
Examples:
  - apatani/hari: Tara apiñ dw[do] = Tara is eating food
  - apatani/hari: Mólù yasañ ta'[do] = They are chopping firewood (with an axe)
  - apatani/hari: Mwdó hu[do] = Rain is falling (It's raining)
